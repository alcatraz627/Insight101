Workshops
The day when you actually get out with a khakee uniform and learn real engineering. Workshop will get you a proper hands on training on the engineering aspects. It happens during your vacations where you feel like not leaving home. But it is totally worth it.
Workflow
Course registration, Grades, Makeup exams, Transcript, document upload - this is where you get all this and a lot more done.
Course Registration
At the end of every semester, the course registration portal opens for new courses to be taken next semester. You will be provided with your core course list, your elective list and also your pending courses list which you are supposed to select and later needs to be approved by fac ad.
Electives
The extra courses which you feel like taking which may be or may not be related to your core branch. Some times these are compulsory to take and sometimes you have a choice of selecting.
Life skills and ID
A course of totally different league. Every engineer needs a leadership and creative and this one fulfills all of them. It is the actual fun course where you will never feel bored. Because it is the only where you will see movies and learn from them. Sounds fun right?
