Room Sharing
In your freshmen year, single room will be shared by two people. From second year onwards your will be alloted new hostels where most of the people are given are single rooms and some still have shared but bigger rooms. Also you can apply for hostel change at the start of the semester with valid reason.
Techroom
This wouls be the messiest place in your hostel.You will find almost every tool here because this is the place where all the techsoc related bota and machines are made.
Library
Other than central library there is also your hostel library where you can find some good novels and chosen books over the years by your seniors. You can issue the book here for whole sem if no one needs it.
Music Room
You will find this in almost every hostel.If you are a music enthusiast and have a good feel for playing instruments then you can try a hand over here.
TV room
You wont find a place in here during a cricket or a football match. This room has a television set(default is bravia) and a good sound system. Also, a subscription of channels in every language is a plus point.
Study Room
Not present in all the hostels and in some it is the library only. Place where you will find people from your hostel doing group study during your quizzes and Exams.
Sports
Friendship in IIT bonds up very fast over sports. And you have a general sports equipment room so that you have some active evenings playing with your friends in hostel quadi.
Other Common Rooms
These are some other miscellaneous common room which were added on request. Even I dont know about them.
