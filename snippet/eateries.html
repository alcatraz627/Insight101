Himalaya Mess
Yes the mess.Three floored buiding with six caterers. You have an option to choose among the caterers. A reliable an option whenever you feel hungry. Timely meal routines with heavy breakfast makes up your day.
Zaitoon
On the ground floor of Quark, opposite Saraswati hostel- your non vegetarian seniors swear by this as their saving grace on campus. Insti’s default conference hall for every student meeting ever. Whole meals for decent rates.
Cafe Coffee Day
That the rates are subsidised on campus is the first thing you’ll notice. Near the girl’s hostels and the library. It’s CCD, you know the food.
Gurunath
You can literally find everything here from dosa to juices and pizza. One of the best place to have your intellectual discussions after classroom sessions.
Campus Cafe
The canteen in the academic zone, on the road between Central Workshop / CFI and the Hospital. Quick save for when you have a morning free slot and have missed breakfast. Tasty (and cheap) South Indian breakfast and lunch.
Suprabha
On the SAC/ CCW road. Open almost 24x7. ! pure vegetarian ! A really huge menu, brilliant quality, and decent rates make up for that.
Zea-PutGrub
At Himalaya- The Pizza/Pasta/Dessert places on campus. PutGrub also has a website for online orders, which they deliver to your hostel until midnight.
Ramu(Girls & Boys)
At Himalaya. The most lit place at night (we mean till 6am). Double coffee, one of the 6 types of chai, nutella sandwich, and family packets of Lays are what will get you through exams. Wolf nights, Contact, and Lit(erature) will get you through the rest.
Andavar
Name any fruit and you will find juices and shakes for that. This place has more than 60+ types of juices in their menu. Also every day combo offer gets you a good deal if you come with your friends.
Bru
At Himalaya. Paneer roll, dry noodles, lemon iced tea. For if you (choose to) miss dinner in the mess. They also have an outlet in Jamuna hostel.
Taramani Gate
Anytime you feel like eating Biryani, this is the place you crave for. Outside the gate the shops not only have very cheap options for food but also the tasty ones. 'Supriya and 'mummy daddy'' will cover almost everything you need.
Krishna Gate
Chaat lovers always have their special spot and here they have too. Shops outside K gate will get you good north Indian food and specially very good chaat. A place to go and have a tea when you take a walk with your friends.
Ascendas
This place has Domino's. I think that's enough.
