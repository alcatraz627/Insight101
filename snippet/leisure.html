Elliot Beach
Also known as Besant nagar beach, This is the best place to spend your evening. Only a couple of kilometres away from main gate, this place has a unique touch of nature. Sea side sunset and a calm beach always relaxes you up.
Marina Beach
Asia's largest beach awaits you here. A huge sea side with plethora of shops for food(specially fish)and games. It is a bit crowded spot but you find it relaxing and a good place to spend time with family and friends.
Phoenix mall
Up for a movie!! You have a couple of multiplexes nearby(PVR in grand mall and Luxe in phoenix mall).Also along with that phoenix is a good place to hang out and shop.
Roam Around Campus
Get to know more about your college. You may have your bicycle, just ride wherever you want and explore the beauties of the campus. And you will find many I ensure you that!
Himalaya Lawns
A bit lazy to go out! But still want to spend some peaceful time. Himalaya lawns is the place. The default wolf night landmark. Lie on the lush green grass and gaze at the stars in the sky.
