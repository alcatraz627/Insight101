Classes & Labs
The orientation starts with HOD telling you about the department. Then your branch counsellor will brag about the total number of labs and classes in the department and they will take you around all of them and also what you will be learning.
Classroom Complex
The first place where you will attend all your classes. Also all the electives which are not related to your branch.
Central Library
You will witness a stark difference in the usage of library as compared to libraries outside campus. Learn the procedure properly or else you will never get it and you would end up running here and there. Also you will see the enourmous number of books the library holds.
HPCE
The fancy name for the supercomputer placed at the PG Senapathy Centre for Computing.
LDAP & smail
LDAP is your institute digital ID. You cannot access anything in insti if you dont know your LDAP. Also smail is the internal institute mail where you will get to know about everything that is happening in insti like important announcements, any events(mostly events), scholarships and a lot. Also it provides you unlimited cloud storage unlike gmail.
