Shroeter
A sports tournament where all the hostels compete against each other and try to perform best in every sports activity. The points then add up and the hostel with highest points win.
Lit-Soc
LitSoc is the annual inter-hostel culturals competition. Very competitively fought through the year with competitions on literally everything extracurricular- music, dance, dramatics, comedy, writing, filmmaking, photography, design, word games, an occasional ridiculous one like ‘Human Foosball’, quizzing, oratory events, the big ones that get entire hostels working like assembly lines- our favourite,'Queen of Sheeba’- and so on.
Tech-Soc
A series of tech competitions for the geeks. You get to build you own bots which will then compete against one another; race against others in Rubik's challenge, solve reverse coding challenges, and so much more.
