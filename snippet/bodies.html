International & Alumni Relations
They will help you getting sem exchange. Are incharge of all the international affairs and a window for alumnus to have a look back into their college. They get us funding(yes a lot of funding).Also they make content for institute.
Saarang
Yes the cultural festival.If you are a cord you will be jam packed for continuous four days maybe you wont even get sleep. If you are not you will have the best feel you have had in your life. No words to explain it just Saarang.
Shaastra
For all the Tech enthu junta this is you festival. You will witness a lot of Tech Battles during four days of Shaastra. Also for Non-Techies shaastra has a lot of fun events. Envisage is one such mesmerizing event.
Centre for Innovation
'Walk in with an idea, walk out with a product'. Yes with that motto they are actually the innovation lab of IIT Madras.They take up student project and take them to international competitions or some turn into successful tech startups.Best place if you want to be real engineer.
Entrepreneurship Cell
With the burst of startup culture in India, most of the engineers today are inclined towards entrepreneurship. E-CELL IITM is dedicated towards developing entrepreneurial eco-system in insti by providing student a platform to jump from 'Idea' to a 'Product'. Yes they serve startups.
Extra Mural Lectures
Extra Mural Lectures is a lectures organizing team in IIT Madras which has a long and remarkable history of hosting highly distinguished speakers like His Holiness The Dalai Lama, Shri Vishwanath Anand, Honourable Minister Suresh Prabhu, Director of Baahubali SS Rajamouli and many others.
