Quiz 1
The first exam bomb that you will encounter when you start the course. It is the first time when you will get the feel of writing exam in IIT. Almost everytime it constitutes 20% of the total final grade of that course.
Quiz 2
Since it is not very far away from End Semester Exams, so it helps you get an idea of the kind of grasp you have before the actual EndSems.
End Semester
Usually with the most weightage. Three hour long exam. Mark the end of the course.
Mid semester
Some courses may omit Quizzes and only hold Mid Semester exams. Some may have both(depending on the prof). Generally there for Lab courses.
Tutorials
Weekly/Bi-Weekly affair. Designed to test you with what has been taught in the last week.
Assignments
Some professors would rather have you try some graded problems your self and turn them in, rather than solve examples in Tutorials.
