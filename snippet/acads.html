Quiz 1
The first exam bomb that you will encounter when you start the course. It is the first time when you will get the feel of writing exam in IIT. Almost everytime it constitutes 20% of the total final grade of that course.
Quiz 2
The second exam in the quiz series. Its same as the quiz 1 itself same weightage, same pattern. But different syllabus.
End Semester
The final examination which decides the fate of your grade for that course. It wovers full syllabus like those old school finals. And it will add upto 50-60% of weigtage in final grade.
Mid semester
For some of the courses(mostly lab courses), there are no quizzes but they are replaced by a mid term exam which happens obviously in the mid of the semester.
