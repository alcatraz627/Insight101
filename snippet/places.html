Gajendra Circle
Gajendra Circle is a landmark of IIT- M that has been held dear by every generation of students that has passed through insti’s gates. In fact, one can almost say it’s the very center of IIT–M’s existence, both literally and figuratively.
Velachery Gate
Velachery Gate is second most used gate after the main gate. This gate opens up into a vast marke where you can find anything ranging from malls,shops,grocery shops and restaurants. You can reach here using institute bus.
Gymkhana
Organisation responsible for all the non-academic things in institute, ranging from sports to hostel and mess. Almost every sport in gymkhana building is free for students(only some need a paid membership card).Taramani guest house is in front of Gymkhana and it also has a bus-stop exactly opposite to it.
IC&SR Building
Home of IAR and ICSR hall. Almost every lecture or seminar event in institute happens here. This building also has NPTEL hall,NIT pallakad office and many more official departments.
Institute Hospital
Equipped with updated medicinal technology. Open for OPD during day time and emergencied are taken during night. 24x7 ambulance, an apollo medical centre and medall healthcare are prominent features in hospital. So anytime you feel sick you can call in an ambulance.
Central Lecture theatre
You will see this place frequently in your starting months, because almost all the institute bodies hold their orientation session in CLT. As the name suggests,it is a theatre but with Air Condition. Many seminars and lectures also take place here.
Open Air Theatre
This is where Proshows happen. This place has the highest capacity of holding crowd in institute. Every weekend there will be a movie screening(mostly latest),all you need is a film card.
Classroom Complex
You will be attending almost all your first year classes here.
Cycle Repair
Having a bicycle in insti is must. And if you have it you need repairs too. You will find shops fro repair near tapti hostel and sangam stadium, but the best way to your cycle repaired is using Caval(its an institute startup).
Heritage Center
Place where you can dig up history back. Look into the past of IIT-M and see how it evolved into a prestigious institute today.
Departments
Almost all the departments have their own seperate buildings. Each department will keep an orientation session before commencement of classes and they will guide you to every part of it and what will you study in next 4/5 years.
ATMs
If you run out of cash, then institute has ATMs at different places. Banks that have ATMs in insti are Canara Bank, State Bank of India, ICICI Bank. If you still dont find any cash then you can go out of taramani or velachery gate and find many ATMs in close radius.
