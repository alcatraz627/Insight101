Placements are an integral part of life at the Institute, one of the paramaters by which you are judged upon by the outside world.These are the most stressful, but very exciting days once you get placed. Placements start on December 1st with a total of 500 companies and a good mix of profiles: Analytics, Consult, Finance, Coding and Core. The highest package varies from 40 - 50 LPA for international roles and the average salary is 10 LPA. Approximately 80 percent of the undergraduate students who have registered will be recruited by the companies. Companies also recruit students in the prefinal year for internships and give Pre Placement Offers which sum up to 60 - 70 offers.
